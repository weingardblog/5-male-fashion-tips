5 male fashion tips

DRESS LIKE A MAN

As most of the men pretend to look youthful, they approach casual styling in the wrong way. If you prefer to look well-dressed, you should aspire to look cool and mature.


Avoid anything overly flashy like printed t-shirts, cartoon tees, or tees with funny quotes that make you look like a teenager and not cool. Graphic tees are also not recommended, although the widespread belief they do not make you look edgy.


Instead, you can opt for solid tees or striped tees or polo tees, which are simple yet do not compromise on class.


CHANGE IT UP

Jeans are great with a look and durability kind of stuff, but at the end of the day, they look the same, they are not the only options for legwear. And they are also common among most of the men who make you blend among them.


You can pull off many outfits that look good on jeans, even with a couple of chinos. It just doubles the number of outfits you can create. An essential pair of camel or grey, along with colored chinos, will help you out.


Wearing them below your waist makes you look odd. You can even opt for subdued colors like olive green or burgundy.


CLASS UP YOUR FOOTWEAR

Footwear is also an essential aspect of your dressing. Your dirty and beaten up sneakers will ruin a great outfit. You might need to replace them with something more distinguished.


Consider a pair of brown leather shoes or boots instead of your sneakers, which make you look elegant. There is much other footwear that goes well with casual outfits like boat shoes, leather loafers, desert boots, ankle boots; few formal leather shoes also go well with casual outfits.


They are cozy to wear and look better than sneakers, which make a perfect combo with casuals. Get yourself a pair you wouldn’t regret it.


DECORATE YOUR WRISTS

Do not leave your wrists naked because they are stodgy. Accessories are the best way to spruce up your outfit. You can consider wearing a watch, a leather bracelet or anything but not a naked wrist.


It adds a bit of charm and gives an identity for your outfit. If you have an expensive watch well and good, else a simple bracelet also does the job. But make sure you cover the most surface of your wrist, do not opt of anything with thin straps or small dials they are pointless to wear.


LAYER YOUR CLOTHES

Layering makes a dull outfit more interesting. You do not need to struggle to match the layers of your clothing; simple tricks would do the job.


Consider adding a shirt under your sweater or wearing a casual shirt over a plain tee or wearing a scarf around your neck; you can add more layers depending on your outfit.


But a jacket or blazer an overcoat always elevates your casual look. Any casual outfit with a layer on top makes you look much better than you think. Do not make it too complicated either; keep it simple yet graceful.
